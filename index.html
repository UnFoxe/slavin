SLAVIN

A few words about me

Вокалист, гитарист, продюсер, автор песен в проектах: Zen City, междуснами, Slavin, Членский Взнос. Сессионный гитарист/вокалист, преподаватель.

Мультижанровость, или музыкальная "биполярка"?

Мне невероятно трудно оставаться в рамках одного стиля даже в одном альбоме собственной группы. Поэтому проектов у меня несколько и вы можете, послушав песни, определить их стили и жанры.

МЕЖДУСНАМИ

The unconscious and the conscious are mixed between sleep and reality every time we fall asleep.

In "betweendreams", personal experiences and memories are so mixed up - in nervous texts, fears, anxieties and joys - in hypnotic, deceptively light guitars. And at the same time, there is an obvious reversal from loud rock towards lyrical pop music with a electronic sound.

ZEN CITY

Zen City - The Heavy guitar Riff got lyrical Vocals as partners. A classic action movie with shootouts of philosophical texts in Russian and English, full of special humor and the indispensable self-irony of the author.

Sometimes, the heroes are helped by a violin quartet, a children's choir, or a crowd of DJ's with acid synths.

On stage, all this is realized in the format of a rock trio, sometimes a quartet and sounds uncompromisingly powerful!

SLAVIN

Slavin - instrumental compositions woven from threads of various genres - be it country, rock or funk.

Despite the fact that this is a project, primarily by a guitarist, the phrase "guitar music" is intentionally absent here.

The guitar here is not a goal, but a means, and Slavin is a set of colors with which the listener draws cartoons or pictures in his mind, serious or not.
